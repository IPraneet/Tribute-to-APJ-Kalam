Tribute to APJ Abdul Kalam

Who is APJ Abdul Kalam ?

Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam was an Indian aerospace scientist

aerospace scientist . A.P.J. Abdul Kalam served as president of the

Republic of India from 2002 to 2007. As president, Kalam

promoted the advancement of the national nuclear weapons

program. Kalam also devised a 20-year action plan to achieve

economic growth through technological development in India.

His sudden Death...

APJ Abdul Kalam died on Monday, July 27 after suffering a cardiac arrest at IIM Shillong.

He died while he was interacting with IIM students which have

always been his favorite thing in the world.

His Death was Shocking to the whole nation.
